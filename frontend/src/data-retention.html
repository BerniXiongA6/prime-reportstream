Data retention

Due to the presence of personally identifiable information or personal health information, ReportStream will not be a permanent repository for reported test data.

Each day's report will be held for 7 days, and wil lbe accessable through the application for the duration of that period. After 7 days has passed for each individual report, the file will eb permanently deleted and will not be recoverable.

Please download data and ingest it into your systems as soon as possible.
