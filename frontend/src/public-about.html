ReportStream is an open source, cloud based platform that aggregates and delivers COVID-19 test results to health departments. We send data directly from testing facilities, labs, and more through a single connection, helping to streamline public health reporting.

Download PRIME 1-pager Contact us
ReportStream (also known as PRIME ReportStream) was created by the CDC and the U.S. Digital Service (USDS) as part of the Pandemic-Ready Interoperability Modernization Effort (PRIME). PRIME is a multi-year collaboration to strengthen data quality and information technology systems in state and local health departments. The U.S. Digital Service is a group of technologists from diverse backgrounds working across the federal government to transform critical services for the people. Specialists join for tours of civic service to create a steady influx of fresh perspectives. USDS continues its non-partisan mission by bringing better government services to all Americans through design and technology.
